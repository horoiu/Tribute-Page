Scooter, a German techno trio that got its start in Hamburg in 1993, is known for fast beats, non-political - and sometimes nonsensical - screaming lyrics. The band's high-energy shows featuring lasers and pyrotechnics.

From the beginning, the band was composed out of three members. The present composition is (from left to right):

  • Michael Simon - producer/ composer/ keyboards, worked for about 10 years as a thechnician for Scooter and he officialy joined the band in 2006;
  • JP Baxxter - vocals/guitar, is the founding member;
  • Phil Spacer - producer/ composer/ keyboards, joined the band in October 2013;

German rave machine Scooter are one of the most successful dance acts of all time, the trio has released 19 studio albums, 5 compilation albums and 4 live albums, and completed numerous international tours.

The group is the embodiment of dance music at its most over the top and excessive, taking the KLF's concept of stadium house to its extreme. Led by austere frontman H.P. Baxxter, the majority of the group's songs feature gigantic singalong choruses (often referencing a wide spectrum of both pop and underground hits), crowd noise, and the loudest & most booming beats imaginable.

Throughout the years, they scored dozens of hit singles and full-lengths, gathered more than 80 Gold & Platinum awards and they have sold over 30 million records worldwide.

This year they are celebrating 25 years of activity, therefore they are organizing a big worldwide event: "100% SCOOTER - 25 Years Wild and Wicked tour".
